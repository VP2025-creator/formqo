Frequently Asked Questions

What is Formqo?

Formqo is an AI-powered form builder that lets you create beautiful, conversational forms in minutes.

Is Formqo free to use?

Yes! Formqo offers a free forever plan with up to 3 forms and 100 responses per month.

What AI features does Formqo offer?

Formqo's AI can generate smart questions, auto-detect input types, create logic branching, and perform sentiment analysis.

What integrations does Formqo support?

Formqo integrates with Slack, Zapier, Webhooks, HubSpot, Notion, Airtable, Google Sheets, Mailchimp, and more.

How much does Formqo cost?

Free at £0/month, Pro at £19/month, Business at £49/month. All paid plans include a 14-day free trial.

Can I remove Formqo branding?

Yes, branding can be removed on Pro and Business plans. Custom domains are also available.

Is Formqo secure?

Yes. SOC 2 compliant with encryption at rest and in transit. Business plans include SSO, SAML, and audit logs.

Does Formqo support file uploads?

Yes, respondents can upload files. Available on Pro and Business plans.
